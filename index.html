About Our college

A Few Words About the College

The Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society’s Polytechnic, Gulbarga was established in the year 1986 with the intention to impart technical education to the peoples of this region. Right from inception, these institutions earned good reputation and always strive for upliftment of people in this region.

The main vision of our institution is to constantly monitor the quality of education & strive towards achieving the transformation of this institution into a novel institution in this area.
